About Dr. Kalam

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist who served as the 11th President of India (2002–2007). Known as the "Missile Man of India" for his work on ballistic missile and launch vehicle technology, he inspired millions through his dedication to education and innovation.

Beyond his scientific achievements, Dr. Kalam was a beloved teacher and author, encouraging young people to dream big and work hard. His humility, discipline, and commitment to the nation's progress continue to influence students and professionals worldwide.

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action." — A.P.J. Abdul Kalam
